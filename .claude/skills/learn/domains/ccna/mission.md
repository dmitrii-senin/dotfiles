# Mission: ccna

## Why
Pass the **CCNA 200-301** exam. It's a concrete cert with a fixed blueprint and a target
date — the goal is exam readiness across all six domains, not open-ended networking depth.
Subnetting fluency and spaced retention of facts are the two things that decide the result,
so they're daily habits.

## What success looks like
- Score comfortably above passing on full 60-question mock exams (`/ccna quiz mock`).
- **Subnetting on reflex** — network/broadcast/range, mask-for-N-hosts, VLSM,
  summarization, wildcard masks, IPv6 — fast and correct (`/ccna subnet` daily).
- Durable recall of the OCG card bank via spaced review (`/ccna flash review` daily).
- No weak domain left behind — weak-area quizzes drive the ledger toward zero.

## Deadline
**Exam ~2026-11-23** (Week 30 of the plan that started 2026-05-04). Pace is set by the
schedule in `ccna.md`; flashcards inject as each chapter's weeks pass.

## Constraints / style
Stay on the blueprint — don't teach off-syllabus material. Cisco/IOS framing (no
cross-domain analogies unless asked). Daily: a subnet drill + due flashcards. Weekly: a
weak-area quiz. Mock exams as the date approaches.
